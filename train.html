focus trainer · adaptive self-suggestion + Stroop

Runs alongside the flicker. Reads your state (HR + aim, same feeds) and delivers brief self-suggestion cues to hold you in the optimal arousal band (performance peaks at moderate arousal — the Yerkes-Dodson curve). The Stroop block measures cognitive control between matches.

Honest framing: this is self-suggestion + arousal regulation, not clinical hypnosis and not an IQ raiser. Any gain is most likely a state/expectancy effect on this specific task — which is real and worth measuring, but is not a durable change in fluid intelligence. Don't run the suggestion audio while driving or anything that needs full attention; gaming is fine.
